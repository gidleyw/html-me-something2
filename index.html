The Fall

Furniture stores have furniture. Grocery Stores have food. I don't think about ice cream at night. I don't make the rules up. I follow them when I can. 14 steps to the top.

I told him no, I don't want to play - but ask me again and I'll have to hand you that L real quick. He did and I'm too competitive to say no. He went for a layup - I moved out the way and next think I remember is my ankle received the L. I forgot what this felt like it has been so long.

I can still walk, I can get to the top of these steps. Every step hurts so much but I need to get my laptop to do my homework. Looks like I need to go up the steps... One step at a time...
